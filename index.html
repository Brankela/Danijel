Inflow is a central European consulting and investment company, which seeks to support investors, companies, government representatives as well as society in general with the innovation and adaption of decentralised systems and security-solutions. Beside of consulting and business development, Inflow is working on providing education-material and security-solutions, to keep our customers and partners save from potential threats arising with the distribution of decentralised systems and currencies.
